About Us

Growing Roots,
Building Futures

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space nestled in the heart of Abuja. Designed for children ages 0 to 10, we offer a safe, joyful environment where learning happens through exploration, creativity, and play.

From stay-and-play sessions to our homeschool hub and community support for parents and caregivers — CFC is more than a centre; it's a family. We believe every child deserves space to grow at their own pace, surrounded by nature, love, and intention.

Our Mission

To create a nurturing, nature-inspired learning community where children aged 0–10 flourish through play-based education, creative exploration, and authentic connection — while empowering families with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to raise conscious, confident children.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every child grows up connected to nature, celebrated for who they are, and surrounded by intentional communities that uplift the entire family. We envision Abuja's leading family-centred space where learning is joyful, families are supported, and childhood is truly honoured.

What We Offer

Our Programs & Activities

Thoughtfully designed experiences that spark curiosity, build confidence, and nurture every stage of early childhood.

⭐ Core Programs
🏡
Stay & Play Sessions

Open, drop-in playgroup sessions for babies, toddlers and young children. Parents and caregivers are welcome to stay, connect, and relax while little ones explore in a safe, stimulating environment. Just joyful, child-led play.

📚
Homeschool Hub & Community

A structured yet flexible learning community for homeschooling families. Children benefit from social interaction, curriculum-linked activities, and expert facilitation — while parents gain a supportive network of like-minded educators.

👩‍🍼
Nanny Intensive Training

A professional development programme for caregivers and nannies covering child development, first aid, conscious caregiving, age-appropriate play, and effective communication with families.

🚀
Holiday Camps & S.T.E.A.M

Action-packed holiday programmes featuring Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts & Mathematics through immersive experiences. Scavenger hunts, messy play, and creative challenges make learning irresistibly exciting.

🌿 Core Activities
🌲
Forest School & Outdoor Play

Child-led exploration in natural settings. Building, climbing, observing wildlife, and connecting deeply with the natural world.

🎨
Creative Arts & Crafts

Painting, sculpting, collage, and mixed-media projects that develop fine motor skills and encourage self-expression.

🎯
Weekly Enrichment Clubs

Themed weekly clubs — storytelling, music, science experiments, cooking — keeping every week fresh and exciting.

🚌
Waka Wednesday Excursions

Weekly field trips around Abuja that bring learning to life — museums, markets, parks become classrooms.
