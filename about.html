About Conscious Family Centre

A safe nurturing space where children learn through play and families grow together.

Nature Connected Learning

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10.

We provide stay-and-play sessions, homeschool support, community connection and creative learning experiences that encourage confidence, curiosity and intentional growth.

Our environment is calm, family-centered and designed to help children learn naturally through exploration, creativity and play.

Through outdoor activities, enrichment clubs, creative arts and intentional experiences, we help families create meaningful childhood memories while supporting emotional and educational growth.
